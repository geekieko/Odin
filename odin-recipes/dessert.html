Tiramisu Cheesecake

Tiramisu -flavoured cheesecake combines the flavours and richness of tiramisu and NY Cheesecake.

Just before serving, grate some semisweet chocolate on the top

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package ladyfingers
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oeven to 350 degrees. Place a pan of water on the bottom of the oven.
  2. Crush the package of ladyfingers to fine crumbs. Mix the melted butter into crumbs. Moisten with 2 tablespoons of the coffee liqueur.
